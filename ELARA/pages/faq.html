Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

How long do ELARA perfumes last?

Our Eau de Parfum concentrations typically last 8-12 hours on skin, with some of our more intense fragrances lasting even longer. The longevity can vary based on skin chemistry, climate, and application.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened and unused products. For opened items, we provide store credit. All returns must be in original packaging with proof of purchase.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. International orders may be subject to customs fees and import taxes.

Are ELARA perfumes cruelty-free?

Absolutely. We are committed to cruelty-free practices and never test on animals. All our ingredients are ethically sourced.

How should I store my perfume?

Store in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures. Keep the cap tightly closed when not in use to preserve the fragrance.

Can I get samples before purchasing?

We offer sample sets that allow you to try multiple fragrances. Samples are also included with every full-size purchase.

Still have questions? We're here to help.
